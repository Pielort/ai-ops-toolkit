# Steven’s AI Ops Workflow Pack (Free)

## How to use this pack
Each workflow has:
- **Input** (what you paste into the AI)
- **Output** (what you should get)
- **Guardrail** (what to avoid)

> Tip: Don’t ask the model to “be smart.” Give it constraints: format, audience, length, and what NOT to do.

---

## 1) Client Onboarding → Clean Scope + Deliverables
**Input prompt:**
Paste: client name, industry, goals, constraints, deadline, budget, existing assets.
Ask: “Return a one-page scope with deliverables, timeline, assumptions, out-of-scope.”

**Output:** One-page scope doc.

**Guardrail:** No vague deliverables. Force measurable outputs.

---

## 2) Discovery Call Notes → Follow-up Email (Same Day)
**Input:** raw notes or transcript.

**Output format:**
- 3-bullet recap
- 5 decisions
- 5 action items (owner + date)
- Proposed next meeting time

**Guardrail:** No promises beyond what was agreed.

---

## 3) Proposal Builder → 3 Options (Good/Better/Best)
**Input:** scope + business goal.

**Output:**
- Option A/B/C price anchors
- Deliverables + timeline per option
- Risk + assumptions

**Guardrail:** Keep add-ons explicit; avoid bundle confusion.

---

## 4) SOP Generator → Task → Steps → QA Checklist
**Input:** “We do X. Here’s how.”

**Output:**
- Purpose
- Prereqs
- Steps
- QA checklist
- Failure modes

**Guardrail:** Include verification steps, not just steps.

---

## 5) Client Reporting → Metrics → Narrative → Next Steps
**Input:** metrics table + campaign context.

**Output:**
- What changed
- Why it likely changed
- What we’ll do next
- Questions for client

**Guardrail:** Don’t over-claim causality.

---

## 6) Content Pipeline → Idea → Hook → Script → Edit Notes
**Input:** audience + offer + topic.

**Output:**
- 5 hooks
- 1 chosen hook expanded
- 60–90s script
- B-roll/edit notes

**Guardrail:** No clickbait promises; keep claims defensible.

---

## 7) Website Audit → Quick Wins List
**Input:** paste homepage copy.

**Output:**
- 10 quick wins (impact vs effort)
- rewritten hero section (3 variations)

**Guardrail:** Keep voice consistent; don’t change positioning without reason.

---

## 8) Outreach → Personalized Cold Email (Non-cringe)
**Input:** target profile + what you offer.

**Output:**
- 3 subject lines
- 90–120 word email
- soft CTA

**Guardrail:** No spammy urgency; no fake personalization.

---

## 9) Task Triage → “Do / Delegate / Automate / Delete”
**Input:** paste task list.

**Output:** table with rationale.

**Guardrail:** Automation needs stop conditions.

---

## 10) Postmortem → What Broke + Fixes
**Input:** describe the failure.

**Output:**
- Root cause hypotheses
- Tests to confirm
- Fix plan
- Prevent recurrence checklist

**Guardrail:** Don’t blame the model; fix the system.
